Soccer

Joga Bonito. I have been playing it for 25 years. The most popular sport in the World. A competitive game only needs two players. You don't have to be the biggest and fastest to be good. You only need to be smart and coordinated. Every four years I dedicate two months to the most popular televised event the world has ever known. If you know the game or play the game, you have rapport with 80% of people around the world. Some of my closest friends I have met through the game. This is the sport that brings people together.

I also have the privilege of volunteering with United East Athletic Association, teaching youth the beautiful game.
